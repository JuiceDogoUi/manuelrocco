# Case Study Structure

**Target:** 280-350 words / 2-3 min read

## Sections

### 1. The Hook (30-40 words)
Surprising metric or outcome that forces them to keep reading.

### 2. The Puzzle (50-70 words)
What was broken and why it mattered. Include 1-2 metrics showing the gap.

### 3. Your Decision (60-80 words)
One pivotal choice. Show why you chose this path despite alternatives.

### 4. What Changed (80-100 words)
3-5 specific changes with outcomes. Format: "Changed X to Y because Z"

### 5. The Result (40-60 words)
Primary metric first. Include secondary metrics, scale, and what this enabled next.

### 6. What I'd Do Differently (30-50 words)
One honest reflection on how you'd improve the process.

---

## Rules

- No em dashes
- Active voice throughout
- Numbers and metrics wherever possible
- Specific over generic
- Each section self-contained (scannable)

---

## Categories

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Built** | Products I shipped end-to-end |
| **Led** | Teams and systems I drove |
| **Designed** | Solutions that moved metrics |
