# Positioning Quick Reference

## By Role Type
- **Lead/Manager**: Emphasize team, strategy, stakeholders. De-emphasize IC work.
- **Senior IC**: Emphasize craft, speed, ownership. De-emphasize management.
- **Design Engineer**: Emphasize code, prototyping, technical. De-emphasize pure visual.

## By Company Stage
- **Early startup**: Speed, scrappiness, ambiguity comfort
- **Scale-up**: Process, systems, quality at scale
- **Enterprise**: Stakeholder navigation, influence, complexity

## Default Positioning
"Design leader who bridges product strategy and hands-on craft, building teams that ship measurable outcomes."
